About CareLink

PUPBC CareLink is a dedicated Clinical Management System developed for the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Biñan Campus. We focus on digitizing health records to provide students and faculty with efficient, secure, and fast medical services.

  • Paperless record keeping
  • Real-time student consultation history
  • Centralized clinic dashboard
