About the System

PUPBC CareLink is designed to digitalize the campus medical experience. By transitioning from paper-based logs to a secure web-based portal, we ensure that student health records are managed with precision and privacy.

This initiative supports the university's goal of providing better welfare services through efficient IT solutions.
